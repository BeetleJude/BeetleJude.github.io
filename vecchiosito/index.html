The ultimate Warcraft collection has arrived just in time for the holidays. Blizzard Entertainment this week began shipping Warcraft Battle Chest, a three CD-ROM set that includes the number-one selling Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness, the Warcraft II expansion set and the gaming classic, Warcraft: Orcs and Humans.



This limited edition CD-ROM set is the first time the entire number-one selling Warcraft series has been offered in one collection. Warcraft Battle Chest includes both DOS and Macintosh versions of all three titles and is available nationwide. Suggested retail price is $69.95. Customers can call (800) 953-SNOW for additional product and sales information.
